Changwoo BAE, PhD

Hello, I am Changwoo Bae, a mechanical engineer and a physicist.

I received my Bachelor’s and Master’s degrees of Mechanical Engineer in Soft Interface Laboratory at Kyung Hee University advised by Prof. Choongyeop Lee in South Korea. Later, I moved to France and received my PhD degree in Physics at Institut Lumière Matière, Université de Lyon advised by Dr. Anne-Laure Biance.

My primary research focuses on fluid dynamics at interfaces, such as superhydrophilic, superhydrophobic, surfactant laden interfaces, etc.
